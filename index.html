❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Does BestCraft AI work without Gemini Nano?
Yes! Our built-in knowledge base has 200+ Minecraft tips, crafting recipes, mob strategies, and guides. Free users get rule-based answers that are accurate and detailed. Gemini Nano just adds natural-language understanding for Premium users.
Does it support Java Edition and Bedrock?
Yes, BestCraft AI covers both Java Edition and Bedrock Edition up to Minecraft 1.21 (Tricky Trials). Where differences exist, we note them clearly.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. All AI processing uses Chrome's on-device Gemini Nano — your questions never leave your browser. We collect no personal data, require no account, and have no tracking.
How do I enable Gemini Nano?
Go to chrome://flags, search for "Prompt API for Gemini Nano", set it to Enabled, then relaunch Chrome. The extension's onboarding walks you through it step-by-step.
Can I get Premium for free?
Yes! Leave a 5-star review on the Chrome Web Store and claim 30 days of free Premium access. Just click "Review & Claim" in the extension after 10 questions.
